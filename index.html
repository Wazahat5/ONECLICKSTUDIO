PVC Card Printing Studio

A dedicated production tool for real, physical PVC/ID cards — true ISO/IEC 7810 (CR80) dimensions, print-grade bleed, rounded corners, safe-zone guides, crop & registration marks, and duplex sheet layouts.

  • Real card sizes: CR80 85.60 × 53.98mm (portrait/landscape) or custom mm
  • 300 / 600 / 1200 DPI, auto edge-extended bleed, ISO 3.18mm corner radius
  • Actual-size ruler preview + full sheet-layout preview
  • Auto-fit maximum cards per sheet, adjustable margins & gutter
  • 3 duplex modes: side-by-side, separate sheets, mirrored long-edge duplex
  • Export single card or full sheet(s) as PNG or exact-mm PDF, or print directly
